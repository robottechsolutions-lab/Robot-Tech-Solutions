Meet the Founder

👨‍💻
Olan Tony
Founder & Visionary — Robot Tech Solutions

I've always believed that technology isn't just a tool — it's a language, and once you speak it, there are no limits to what you can create.

From tearing apart gadgets as a kid to building full robotics systems today, my obsession with how things work has never stopped. Robotics, drones, AI, autonomous systems — these aren't just fields to me, they're passions I wake up thinking about every single morning.

I started Robot Tech Solutions because I saw a gap: too many brilliant young minds with zero access, zero guidance, and zero community. I want to change that. This movement is built for the curious, the bold, and the builders. If you've got the fire, we've got the fuel.

— We are just getting started. The best is yet to come. 🚀
